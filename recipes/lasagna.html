Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers rich, flavorful components to create a hearty, comforting meal. It features sheets of tender pasta layered with a savory meat sauce or vegetable filling, creamy ricotta or béchamel, and melted mozzarella cheese. Often seasoned with a blend of aromatic herbs like basil and oregano, lasagna is baked to perfection, creating a golden, bubbly top and soft, flavorful layers underneath. This versatile dish can be customized with ingredients like spinach, sausage, or mushrooms, making it a favorite for both traditionalists and culinary adventurers alike. Perfect for family gatherings or special occasions, lasagna is a timeless recipe that brings warmth and satisfaction to the table.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

Cooking Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it into small pieces.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce, tomato sauce, dried basil, oregano, red pepper flakes (if using), and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then remove from heat.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt. Mix well and set aside.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, a layer of meat sauce, and a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese and the remaining Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil if desired, and serve warm.
