Chicken Soup

Description

This comforting chicken soup is a heartwarming classic, perfect for chilly days or when you’re feeling under the weather. Packed with tender chunks of chicken, vibrant vegetables, and flavorful herbs, it’s a bowl full of nourishment and warmth. Whether enjoyed as a starter or a main course, this homemade soup is a wholesome and satisfying dish that’s sure to become a favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 cup egg noodles (or pasta of your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Cooking Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and add the dried thyme, parsley, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Bring to a gentle boil.
  5. Add the shredded chicken to the pot and reduce the heat to a simmer. Let it cook for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Add the egg noodles and cook according to the package instructions, usually 6-8 minutes, until tender.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning with additional salt and pepper, if needed.
  8. Remove the bay leaf before serving.
  9. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
